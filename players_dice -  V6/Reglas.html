SERIOUS MODE: THE GAME

Mission: To accumulate as many points as possible by answering the questions correctly before the round ends.

Preparations

  1. Players: The game is for two players.
  2. Board: A spiral board of 6x6 squares is used, starting at the top left corner and ending at the final square.
  3. Dice: Players roll a die to determine their progress on the board.

How to Play

  1. Turns: Players alternate turns. Player 1 begins by rolling the die.
  2. Rolling the Die:
    • When the die is clicked, a random number between 1 and 6 is generated.
    • The number obtained determines how many squares the player will advance on the board.
  3. Movement on the Board:
    • The player advances his token on the board according to the result of the die.
    • If the player is in a square that is not the final square, he advances according to the number obtained.
  4. Questions:
    • Upon landing on a new square, the player must answer a question.
    • The question is presented with three answer options.
    • The player has to choose the correct option to score a point.
  5. Points:
    • If the player answers correctly, one point is added to his total.
    • If the answer is incorrect, the player receives no points, and the game continues.
  6. End of Game:
    • The game ends when a player reaches the last square on the board.
    • At the end, the final scores of both players are displayed.
  7. Winner: The player who reaches the last square first and accumulates the most points wins.
